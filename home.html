★Everything you can do

Skopi recognizes QR codes, product barcodes, contact cards, WiFi networks, links, phone numbers, emails, and more — instantly. Tap a result and Skopi opens it the smart way: dial the number, join the WiFi, save the contact, or open the link in your favorite browser.

  • Scan QR codes and 1D barcodes (EAN, UPC, Code 128 / 39 / 93, ITF, Data Matrix, PDF417, Aztec)
  • Scan from photos in your library
  • Generate QR codes for URLs, plain text, WiFi, contacts (vCard), email, phone, and SMS
  • Smart actions on every result — open link, call number, send email, join WiFi
  • Local scan history with search and filters
  • Light haptics + scan beep, both toggleable

1Private by design

Skopi never uploads your scans. Your scan history lives on your iPhone, encrypted by iOS. No account, no cloud, no tracking of what you scan.
